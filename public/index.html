03Quality Assurance

Zero-tolerance, by design.

Our commitment to quality is fully in compliance with AS 9100 and ISO 9001 standards, complemented by improved internal quality protocols using advanced AI and ML algorithms. Shivayu Aerospace practices a Continuous Improvement philosophy based on Non-Conformance (NC) analysis and best engineering practices.
